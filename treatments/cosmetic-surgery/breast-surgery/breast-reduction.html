Breast Reduction

Breast reduction is a plastic surgery procedure designed to reduce excessive breast size, reduce breast weight, and reposition saggy nipples. As a result of breast reduction surgery, a more proportional and aesthetically attractive breast appearance is aimed in size and shape.
The operation is performed by removing excess fat, glandular tissue and skin. Many women can benefit from breast reduction surgery and live a healthier, more comfortable life while enjoying a more beautiful, attractive breast appearance.
Unfortunately, breast reduction is not available on the NHS and patients must pay for the procedure out of their own pocket. Patients abroad may consider going abroad as an attractive alternative for plastic surgery in places with more affordable fees than breast augmentation surgeries in their home countries.
For women with extremely large breasts who travel abroad for breast reduction surgery, breast reduction can be a life-changing initiative. Women who have had breast reduction surgery tend to experience improved self-esteem, higher self-confidence, and a better quality of life.
Another advantage of undergoing breast reduction surgery is increased physical activity. They can perform simple actions much more easily, such as bending over to pick up something or buying a new dress.

Breast Lift And Breast Reduction
It is a holistic cosmetic breast contouring treatment designed to fulfill the functions of both procedures (breast reduction and breast lift) in a comprehensive breast reduction and breast lift plastic surgery procedure.
The operation aims to improve the size and shape of the breasts. Breast reduction reduces the size of enlarged breasts by removing excess tissue and skin. Breast lift is done to lift and lift the breasts. When breast reduction and breast lift operations are combined, it can help eliminate problems such as overgrown and sagging breasts:
- Loss of sensation due to tense nerves
- Grooves on the shoulders due to the bra strap
- Skin irritation

Breast reduction and breast lift surgery is performed under general anesthesia. The plastic surgeon will make an incision to lift and reshape the breast tissue.

The Effect of Big Breasts on Women
In addition to aesthetic concerns, heavy breasts can negatively affect women's daily activities. Men suffering from large breasts may feel embarrassed by their appearance as well as back and neck pain, bra strap marks and hunched postures.
Breast reduction is one of the most requested aesthetic breast operations in terms of patient satisfaction, as it provides relief to women suffering from extremely large, heavy breasts.

Breast Reduction Surgery For Men

Some men suffer from overdeveloped breasts similar to female breasts. Gynecomastia surgery can help reduce male breast size and restore the breast to its former appearance. Breast reduction surgery for men is one of the plastic surgery treatments designed to remove breast tissue and excess fat accumulated in the chest area.

Breast Reduction And Breastfeeding
Many women are concerned about the effects of breast reduction surgery on breastfeeding. Fortunately, our plastic surgeons take a very careful approach when performing breast reduction surgery to preserve breastfeeding function as much as possible.
Breast reduction surgery is one of the most commonly used methods in Türkiye. This method gives the best results for women who will breastfeed later. In this approach, the areola is not completely removed and therefore the connection between the nipple and breast canals is left intact. If the nipple is to be completely removed and surgically repositioned in a breast reduction procedure, your breastfeeding status may be compromised.

Candidates Suitable For Breast Reduction Surgery
Suitable candidates for breast reduction surgery are women who want to change the aesthetic and physical effects of large breasts. Ideal candidates for breast reduction surgery should be healthy and have reasonable expectations from breast reduction surgery.
Your plastic surgeon will meet with you at the pre-operative consultation and make a detailed evaluation. He or she will listen to your concerns and talk to you about your aesthetic goals. It will help you decide if this operation is right for you.
Our plastic surgeons in Türkiye can also suggest what size you should have according to your height and weight. When you lose weight after breast reduction surgery, the newly contoured breast shape may lose. For this reason, doctors recommend that patients be close to their ideal weight during breast reduction surgery.
You may be a good candidate for breast reduction surgery if you have one or more of the following symptoms:
- If your large breasts limit your physical activity
- If you experience back, neck or shoulder pain due to heavy breasts
- If your bra strap creates indentations on your body
- If you have skin irritations under your breasts
- If you have saggy or tight breasts
- If the nipples are under the breast crease when unsupported
- If you have a large areola

Breast Reduction Procedure
During the breast reduction procedure, your plastic surgeon will make an anchor-shaped or vertical incision to remove excess tissue, fat, and skin. The areola will be repositioned. The breasts are then stretched and reshaped with the skin around the areola and the incisions are closed.
In some cases, liposuction is required to remove excess fat in the armpit area in breast reduction operations. Sometimes, only liposuction can be used for breast reduction. As is known, breast reduction surgery is performed under general anesthesia and can take between two and four hours. It can also be done as an outpatient.

Breast Reduction With Liposuction
In some cases, breast reduction can only be done using liposuction surgery. Some of the benefits of liposuction in breast reduction are shorter, less surgical procedures, unnoticeable post-operative scars and long-lasting results. However, this breast augmentation technique can only achieve desired results in certain patients.
Patients who want a reasonable reduction in breast size, have appropriate skin elasticity, and require little or no sagging are the best candidates for breast reduction with the liposuction technique.
Patients with excessive breast size and excess fat tissue are ideal candidates for breast reduction with liposuction.

Breast Reduction Techniques
Depending on your aesthetic goals and anatomy, there are several surgical techniques for breast reduction. Plastic surgeons at zetaworld have extensive experience with both the 'traditional' (anchor-shaped scar) model and the relatively new technique known as the 'short vertical scar'.
Each breast reduction method has its pros and cons. Our plastic surgeons will explain different breast reduction techniques during the pre-operative consultation.
All breast reduction surgery techniques reduce the size of the breast and, at the same time, enable the breast to lift and regain its youthful appearance.

Vertical or "Lollipop" Breast Reduction Technique
Generally, female patients who need a reasonable reduction in breast size and have more significant sagging are suitable candidates for the vertical breast reduction technique.
The lollipop breast reduction technique can make two different incisions: one runs around the areola and the other runs vertically under the areola or the crease under the breast. This incision technique allows plastic surgeons to remove excess fat, skin and breast tissue and reshape the new small breast.
A vertical breast reduction technique can leave minimal scars on the breast that are limited only to the area below the nipple. However, these scars can be easily hidden under a bra or bikini.
- Inverted T or “Anchor” breast reduction technique: Inverted T breast reduction has 3 methods of incisions: one around the areola, one vertically from the areola to the breast crease—and finally along the crease under the breast. This breast reduction surgery technique allows for maximum tissue removal and allows breast reshaping.Therefore, plastic surgeons will use this method if a large breast requires reduction or correction, if the breast has significant sagging or asymmetry.
A reverse T or anchor breast reduction surgery scars are similar to vertical breast reduction technique scars.
Regardless of the breast reduction technique used, our expert plastic surgeons will make these incisions as small as possible and with the right post-operative care, these scars will be reduced.

Breast surgeons with years of experience are capable of understanding, anticipating and fulfilling patient expectations in a candid and realistic manner. They have earned a reputation as both skilled and caring plastic surgeons.

Healing Process In Breast Reduction
Breast reduction surgery can be easy and hassle-free if you follow the doctor's instructions.
Our relevant plastic surgeon will give you a detailed explanation of the best and suitable practices for healing breast reduction surgery and maintaining your new appearance. After breast reduction surgery, we will take you to your accommodation and treat you with medication if you experience any discomfort.
It is very important to rest for a few days and avoid all physical activity. If your job does not require physical activity, you can return to work one week to ten days after breast reduction surgery.
Exercise should be avoided for about a month after breast reduction surgery. You need to be careful while wearing your bra for the first 4-6 weeks. The doctor will give you instructions on a good sports bra or clothing that will speed up your recovery.
The results of breast reduction surgery are permanent. However, it is important to consider as factors such as the natural aging process, weight changes or giving birth can affect the results of breast reduction surgery. For this reason, you should discuss the results of breast reduction surgery with your doctor in a preliminary consultation.

Tips For Healing Breast Reduction Surgery:
Post-Surgery Care
After the surgery is complete, your plastic surgeon will wrap the wound in your entire chest area with gauze. This helps protect your wounds from external factors. The doctor may want your bandages to stay wrapped for a week.
Drainage: The doctor will use surgical tubes overnight to drain excess fluids that flow after breast reduction surgery. Drainage may take several hours and swelling will decrease after the procedure.
Surgical Bra: Before you leave the clinic, you will wear a surgical bra and continue to wear it for several weeks to keep your breasts stable in their new shape.
Pain management: As with all surgical procedures, there may be pain and swelling for a few days or weeks after surgery. Bruising and tenderness at the surgical site is a normal phenomenon. Our doctors can prescribe pain medications to relieve symptoms.
Rest and Recovery: While you may feel more comfortable after surgery, it is very important to give your body some time to heal. Therefore, in the weeks after your breast reduction surgery, it is important to take care of yourself and avoid excessive physical activity.
