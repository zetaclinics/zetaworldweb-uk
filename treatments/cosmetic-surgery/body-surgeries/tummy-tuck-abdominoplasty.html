Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)

What Is The Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)?
Abdominal stretching is the operation to tighten the abdomen by removing excess fat, sagging, deformities and excess skin in the abdominal region. As a result of different stretching operations over the centuries, it has become possible to have a healthier and flatter stomach in the light of technological changes and developments on this subject.
 

What Are The Types of Abdominoplasty Surgery?
Depending on the type of surgery to be performed, the doctor usually pays attention to the fact that it will meet the needs of the person and eliminate the sagging. Types of surgery;

1. Full Abdominoplasty
2. Mini Tummy Tuck
 

1- How is Full Abdominoplasty Performed?
It is a type of stretching operation generally performed to eliminate sagging and excessive fat accumulation caused by intense relaxation of the waist area and anterior abdominal wall. The body is exposed to a lot of stretching due to reasons such as experiencing excessive weight loss, rapid weight gain and loss, sagging due to genetic reasons, rapid deformation of the abdominal skin, loosening of the anterior abdominal wall after birth. With this surgery, the subcutaneous fat tissue and excess skin are removed, tightening the muscles, stretching the skin in the abdomen and reconstructing the navel, sagging and loosening are removed. Thus, a more aesthetic appearance is gained in the abdominal region.
2- How is Mini Abdominoplasty Performed?
Mini tummy tuck surgery is an intentional procedure performed as a result of excessive lubrication in the lower part of the abdomen that loses its flexibility, cannot recover and hangs down heavily. Usually, the fat in the lower part of the abdomen is removed with the vaser liposuction method and a small incision is made. The abdominal skin is stretched with an incision made between the umbilicus and the upper groin. With this procedure, the abdomen is stretched without touching the belly button and the tension is increased by pulling the belly button downwards. Then, an aesthetic appearance is created by removing fat from the waist and abdomen. Unlike full abdominoplasty, it is performed in the lower part of the abdomen without touching the belly button, no operation is performed on the upper part.
 

What Are The Points To Be Consider Before Abdominoplasty?
Smoking and alcohol consumption should be stopped at least 2 weeks before the specified surgery date.
If there are vitamins, blood thinners or similar drugs that are used regularly, the doctor should be informed and the intake should be stopped.
Foods such as herbal tea and flaxseed should not be taken before the surgery and care should be taken to eat a vegetable-based diet.
If any infection enters the body, flu, cold, etc., the date of surgery should be postponed.
Before tummy tuck surgery, long-term exercises, sunbathing for a long time and diet types that require low-calorie nutrition should be avoided.
Walking, meditation can be done in the open air, which accelerates healing and provides psychological well-being, and attention should be paid to a balanced diet.
Eating and drinking should be stopped at least 8 hours before the surgery and the recommended diet should be given.
 

How Is Abdominoplasty Performed?
First, a detailed analysis is made with the examination and the doctor decides which technique will be applied. It is determined from which parts of the abdomen the excess skin will be removed. From the area known as the cesarean section among the people, the area to the sides and up to the navel line is drawn. Excess fat and skin that are saggy and cracked are removed from the scratched areas and the skin above is pulled down and stretching is performed. While no intervention is made to the belly button in the mini abdominoplasty operation, the belly button is also intervened in the full abdominoplasty operation and a belly button is created again. Finally, rejuvenating sutures are placed on the incisions and the abdominal muscles are tightened. With the completed operation, instead of soft, sagging, loose and cracked images in the abdomen; the person gains an aesthetic abdomen shape that is alive, tense and curved.
 

What Should Be Considered After Abdominoplasty?
After abdominoplasty, the physical appearance of the body changes completely.
For control purposes, you usually stay in the hospital for 1 to 3 days as prescribed by the doctor.
Showering should not be done until the stitches are removed. This situation varies between 10 days and 20 days.
In order to return to work, it is necessary to rest and recover at home for 2 weeks.
Herbal teas should be consumed with the recommendation of the doctor. Otherwise, it should not be consumed.
Edema-enhancing alcohol and cigarettes should not be taken until recovery is achieved.
Heavy objects should not be lifted and sports activities that require weight should be avoided.
Care should be taken to sleep on your back and not to sleep on your side.
Regular exercise and diet should be done to avoid regaining excess fat.
 

Abdominoplasty Recovery Process
The recovery process of tummy tuck surgery varies according to the person's quality of life and the structure of their metabolism. In the first days, swelling and pain can be seen in the abdomen. However, it passes after a short time. Painkillers and antibiotics recommended by the doctor should not be interrupted. During dressing, movements that will cause the abdomen to move too much should not be done and dressings should be done regularly by someone else. While the stitches on the surface of the skin are removed after 1 week after the surgery, they do not need to be removed as the stitches inside dissolve over time. After a week, a flat stomach does not occur as expected due to bloating. However, with the reduction of swelling, the desired abdominal shape emerges. One week after the surgery, short walks can be done in a light-paced home environment. The healing process can be accelerated by doing light exercises that do not require weight. According to the person's body resistance, full recovery is provided between 6 and 1 years.

When Does Edema After Abdominoplasty Go Away?
Edema usually occurs due to swelling in the human body after surgery. The swelling that occurs in abdominoplasty operations is caused by the body's edema. 80% of the edema passes within 2 months. The remaining edema passes up to one year. Attention should be paid to the consumption of food and beverages that will cause edema, and full recovery should be expected to get definitive results.
 

Pregnancy After Abdominoplasty
After tummy tuck surgery, there is no situation that will prevent the person from getting pregnant, either planned or unplanned. Only during pregnancy, due to factors such as the development of the baby and weight gain, cracks may occur in the abdomen or sagging may appear. However, this situation is likely to be temporary. For this reason, it should give priority to a healthy pregnancy process. If this surgery is planned to be performed after pregnancy, it should be performed at least 1 year after birth. During this period, the person should have a healthy and balanced diet. Thus, the condition of cracks and sagging will be clarified.

Do Postpartum Stretch Marks Go Away With Tummy Tuck?
Tears in the subcutaneous tissue due to pregnancy are called 'cracks'. If these cracks remain under the navel and there is intense fat accumulation, a stretching operation can be performed at least one year after birth to tighten the muscles.
 

What Are The Risks of Abdominoplasty Surgery?
As with all surgical procedures, there are risks in stretching surgeries. Abdominoplasty, which is among the most effective methods of body shaping, as in every surgery; Problems such as blood clotting, infection, fluid accumulation during surgery, and blood accumulation may occur. However, the problem disappears as quick interventions are made. Infections are treated using antibiotics. After the operation, all the risks that may occur by ensuring that the person walks with light steps, using compression stockings and taking medications regularly are eliminated.
 

Is Abdominal Stretching Corset Use Mandatory?
The person starts to use a tummy tuck corset immediately after the operation in order to prevent harmful external effects and to ensure the flatness of the abdomen. It will form a taut layer that intensely protects the abdominal area. The corset should be used for 3 weeks without removing it, even while sleeping. After 3 weeks, if the doctor deems it appropriate, it is either removed or the use of the corset is continued.
 

Will there be any scars in the Abdominoplasty Surgery?
The incisions made in abdominoplasty surgery are usually under the bikini. In the first 3 months, surgical scars appear as scars and appear as cesarean section scars. With the healing of the subcutaneous tissue and the surgical scar removal cream used on its surface, it remains very slightly visible in some areas. At the end of 1 year, the trace appears indistinct. With a healthy diet, sports and diet, excessive lubrication, weight loss, rapid weight gain and loss can be prevented, and the operation scar can be prevented from being permanent. Therefore, care should be taken not to create a trauma to the body.
 

Who Is Abdominoplasty Performed?
Persons over the age of 18,
People with rapid weight gain and loss, visible sagging and loosening after sleeve gastrectomy surgery
If there is intense fat and skin excess in the abdomen,
If the abdominal muscles are relaxed,
Abdominoplasty is performed for people with obvious stretch marks caused by birth.
 

Who Cannot Have Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)?
Especially people with systemic diseases (diabetes, blood pressure, thyroid diseases),
For those who are pregnant,
People with bleeding disorders
People who smoke
It is not appropriate to perform abdominoplasty surgery on people with infections.
 

Is Abdominoplasty Permanent?
After tummy tuck aesthetics, the body takes shape and looks smooth and tight. With the support of diet, sports and healthy nutrition, the permanence of the aesthetic operation is increased. If nutrition is not taken care of after the surgery, deformities begin to occur in the body again. Therefore, care should be taken to exercise.
 

When Does Sexual Intercourse Happen After Abdominoplasty?
For post-operative sexual intercourse, full healing of the subcutaneous and skin surface should be expected. It should be noted that it is between 4 and 6 weeks minimum. Thus, the formation of permanent traumas in the surgical area and vaginal area can be prevented.
 

Which Doctor Performs Abdominoplasty Surgery?
Operations requiring surgical operation; performed by aesthetic, plastic and reconstructive surgery specialists. Therefore, it should be ensured that the doctor who will perform the surgery is a surgeon in his field and it should be paid attention to whether he has experience in this field.
 

Comments of Those Who Have Tummy Tuck Surgery
As in every operation, the comments of those who have had tummy tuck surgery, which includes mini stretching and full stretching operations, are references to those who want to have this surgery. For this reason, criteria such as the comments of the people who had the surgery, the hospital they went to, the doctor chosen, whether they met the expected service should be listened to. Thus, it guides the person in choosing the right health center where the surgery will take place.
