Cookie Policy

The purpose of this Cookie Policy is to inform you about the processing of personal data obtained through the use of cookies during the operation of our website. For cookies other than the mandatory cookies we use on our website, the express consent of the users is obtained and the opportunity to change their consent at any time is provided.

As our users, you can see the types of cookies used on our website through the cookie preference panel, and you can set your preferences with the "on" or "off" options for all cookies except for Mandatory Cookies.

What Is A Cookie?

Cookies are placed on devices (computer, smartphone, etc.) for the purpose of a website working properly, improving user experience, developing and optimizing the site, serving more relevant, interest-based advertising, providing an interesting and personalized website/app and advertising portfolio for visitors. tablets) are small bits of data placed on them.

For more detailed information about cookies, you can visit http://www.aboutcookies.org
 and http://www.allaboutcookies.org

Why Cookies Can Be Used?

Cookies can be used for ensuring the proper performance of the website visit and remembering the information in the next use, improving the functioning of the site, statistical evaluation of site usage, the purposes of presenting the content on the site in the most effective way for you and providing customized services and advertisements to users in line with their preferences and usage habits.

What Are the Types of Cookies?

Cookies, whose main purpose of use is to provide convenience to users, are basically collected in 4 main groups:

Session cookies: These are cookies that allow you to take advantage of various features such as carrying information between internet pages and systematically remembering the information entered by the user, and they are necessary for the functions of the website to function properly.
Performance cookies: These are cookies that collect information about the frequency of visiting the pages, possible error messages, the total time users spend on the relevant page, and the usage patterns of the site, and are used to increase the performance of the website.
Functional cookies: These are the cookies that remind the pre-selected options for the convenience of the user and aim to provide advanced Internet features to the users within the scope of the website.
Advertising and third-party cookies: These are cookies belonging to third-party suppliers and allow the use of some functions on the website and the tracking of advertisements.

Cookies Used on Our Website

Mandatory Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be turned off in our systems. These are usually only set in response to actions taken by you that constitute a service request, such as setting your privacy preferences. You can set your browser to block or warn you about these cookies, but in this case some parts of the site will not work.

Functional Cookies: These cookies allow the website to offer richer functionality and personalization. These may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have included on our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, some or all of these services may not work properly.
Analytical Cookies: Analytical cookies are used to collect information about how visitors use our website. These tools collect information that does not directly identify anyone, including the number of visitors to the website, where the visitors came from and the pages they visited, and share it with us anonymously and cumulatively. The cookies created by these measurement tools have no effect on the operation of the site, and the use of cookies through these tools is subject to your consent.

Advertising and Marketing Cookies: These cookies can be set by our advertising partners through our site. They can be used to build a profile of your interests based on the browsing information they collect from you, which includes uniquely identifying your browser and terminal equipment from third parties.
